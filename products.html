Industries Served

Where Our Products Are Used

Our shell powders are essential raw materials across several high-volume manufacturing industries.

01

Agarbatti / Incense Stick Manufacturing

Shell powder acts as a key filler and binder in agarbatti production, giving structure and a consistent, even burn rate to incense sticks. Our coconut–palm blend is optimised specifically for this application.

02

Mosquito Coil Manufacturing

Used as a slow-burn filler in mosquito coil production, ensuring even combustion, structural integrity of the coil, and effective smoke release. A consistent mesh size is critical, which we guarantee per batch.

03

Briquette & Bio-fuel Manufacturing

Palm and coconut shell powder are compressed into bio-fuel briquettes, a sustainable, high-calorific alternative to coal, used in industrial boilers, brick kilns and biomass energy plants.

04

Rubber & Foundry Industry

Acts as an inert filler in rubber compounds and foundry sand molds. Improves structural density, reduces material cost, and maintains product quality, widely used by tyre and casting manufacturers.

05

Custom & Emerging Applications

We work with buyers across niche applications to supply the right mesh size, blend ratio, and moisture specification. If your process requires a specific formulation, we can accommodate it.
