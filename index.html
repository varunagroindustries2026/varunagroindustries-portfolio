Industries Served

Where Our Products Are Used

01

Activated Carbon Manufacturing

Coconut shell powder is the highest-value feedstock for activated carbon — used in water purification, air filtration, gold recovery and pharmaceutical applications.

02

Agarbatti / Incense Stick Manufacturing

Shell powder acts as a key filler and binder in agarbatti production, giving structure and a consistent, even burn rate to incense sticks.

03

Mosquito Coil Manufacturing

Used as a slow-burn filler ensuring even combustion, structural integrity and effective smoke release. Consistent mesh size guaranteed per batch.

04

Briquette & Bio-fuel Manufacturing

Compressed into bio-fuel briquettes — a sustainable alternative to coal used in industrial boilers, brick kilns and biomass energy plants.

05

Rubber & Foundry Industry

Acts as an inert filler in rubber compounds and foundry sand molds — improves structural density and reduces material cost.

06

Custom Applications

We accommodate niche requirements — specific mesh size, blend ratio and moisture specification tailored to your process.
